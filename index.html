About Me

With a PhD from The University of Texas at Austin and an MS from IIT Kharagpur, I bring over two decades of experience in seismic analysis and geophysical research. I have collaborated with leading organizations like BP, Chevron, and Reliance and continue to contribute to both industry and academia.
